Key insights from Foothill students

Students taking 3+ STEM courses report 40% higher stress (8.2 vs 5.9 avg) and study 6+ more hours per week.
Week 7 (midterms) and week 15 (finals) are the toughest — average study hours nearly double those weeks.
The CS 1A + Math 1A combo is the hardest pairing at Foothill (8.4/10 stress). Swapping one for an elective drops it to 6.1.
Students who attend study groups 2–3× per week earn a 0.31 higher GPA and report 1.4 pts lower stress.
Humanities-only students maintain the highest avg GPA (3.48) with the fewest hours studied (11.2h/week).
